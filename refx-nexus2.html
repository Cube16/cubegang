How to install:

1. Remove files from archive

2. Launch "AiR eLicenser Emulator Setup"

3. Click "Next", "Install", "Done"

4. Open "Nexus 2.iso"

5. Launch "Nexus 2 Setup" and setup it in plugin's folder

6. Open "Nexus content.iso"

7. Remove folder "Nexus content" in plugin's folder

8. Open your music program (Fl Studio, Ableton, Logic Pro,...) and add this plugin

9. Use it :)
